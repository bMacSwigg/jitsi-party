Jitsi Party: Known Issues and Work-arounds

  • Unescapable pop-up about installing the Chrome extension
    • This may pop up in the top-right in every video call, but the X to dismiss it may not work. To fix it, go to meet.jit.si, where you should see the same pop-up. Click the "Do not show me this again" checkbox, then close it. The next time you join a video call, it should be gone.
